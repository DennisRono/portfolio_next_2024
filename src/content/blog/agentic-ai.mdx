# Agentic AI

Agentic AI represents a new paradigm where AI systems operate as autonomous agents capable of planning, reasoning, and executing multi-step tasks with minimal human oversight.

## What is Agentic AI?

Agentic AI refers to AI systems that can:
- Set and pursue goals autonomously
- Break down complex tasks into subtasks
- Use tools and APIs to accomplish objectives
- Learn from feedback and adapt strategies

## Key Characteristics

### Autonomy

Agentic systems can operate independently, making decisions and taking actions without constant human guidance.

### Goal-Directed Behavior

These systems work toward specific objectives, planning and executing sequences of actions to achieve desired outcomes.

### Tool Use

Modern agentic AI can leverage external tools, databases, and APIs to extend their capabilities beyond pure language processing.

## Applications

- Software development automation
- Research assistance
- Customer service automation
- Personal productivity agents

## Challenges and Considerations

Building reliable agentic AI requires addressing challenges in safety, alignment, and predictability to ensure these systems act in accordance with human values and intentions.
